Trades are allowed only through the end of the group stage. Managers must exchange items that preserve roster slot integrity (UEFA for UEFA, CONMEBOL for CONMEBOL, etc. or using the Wild Card).

🔄 Waiver Wire Claim

After the group stage, managers may replace eliminated teams with undrafted remaining teams. Waiver priority goes to the lowest ranking score. Max 1 waiver per manager.

📜 Official League Rules

Roster Structure
  • 1 European team (UEFA)
  • 1 South American team (CONMEBOL)
  • 1 African team (CAF)
  • 1 Asian team (AFC)
  • 1 North American team (CONCACAF)
  • 1 Wild Card team (any confederation, no duplicates, 1.5x score multiplier)
Scoring Structure
  • Group Stage Win: 3 pts
  • Group Stage Draw: 1 pt
  • Goal Scored: +1 pt
  • Clean Sheet: +1 pt
  • Advance to Knockout: +5 pts
  • Round of 32 Appearance: +3 pts
  • Round of 16 Appearance: +5 pts
  • Quarterfinals Appearance: +7 pts
  • Semifinals Appearance: +10 pts
  • Final Appearance: +15 pts
  • Champion Bonus: +25 pts
Disciplinary & Bonuses
  • Penalty Shootout Win: +2 pts
  • Upset Bonus (win vs higher-ranked FIFA opponent): +3 pts
  • Red Card: -2 pts
  • Own Goal: -1 pt
Tiebreakers (Descending Order)
  • Most total wins from drafted teams
  • Most knockout-stage teams
  • Most goals scored
  • World Cup Champion ownership
  • Coin flip

🔥 Firebase Multiplayer Setup

Connect Firebase to enable remote drafting and real-time sync across all 6 managers' devices. Create a free Firebase project at console.firebase.google.com, then paste the firebaseConfig object below.

Quick Setup Guide:
  1. Go to Firebase Console → Create Project
  2. Build → Realtime Database → Create Database → Start in test mode
  3. Project Settings (gear) → Your apps → Web app (</>) → Register
  4. Copy the firebaseConfig object and paste it above
  5. Click "Connect Firebase" → Share this page URL with all 6 managers!
